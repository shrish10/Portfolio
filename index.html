ABOUT

Shristi Murli

I am an enterprising student of Second Year Mass Communication and Journalism (BAMCJ). I’m a Photographer by passion and I always try to use my skills to capture the world through my lens in a unique manner.

My main goal is to work hard and create my own journey in the Media field by creating my own identity through my ideologies and learnings in life.

From my early childhood, my selfless nature of giving has grown into a passion which has paved a way for me to reach out to individuals and organizations in need by providing them with basic necessities like dry ration, clothes, etc. Through this I have tried to initiate a small setup “VAMSH: Vision and Mission for Serving Humanity” with my family to help the underprivileged and people in need through small works of kindness.
